About ViCDaCy Farm

ViCDaCy Farm is a leading agricultural enterprise in Ghana, specializing in sustainable cattle farming. Our mission is to nourish Africa with quality livestock and to promote eco-friendly farming practices that benefit both the community and the environment.

Explore Our Farm

Cattle Farming

Our cattle are raised in open pastures, ensuring they lead healthy and stress-free lives. We prioritize organic feeding practices to produce the finest quality beef.

Crop Cultivation

We cultivate a variety of crops, including maize and soybeans, using sustainable methods that enrich the soil and preserve natural resources.

Our Products

Premium Beef

Our beef products are renowned for their tenderness and flavor, a result of our commitment to quality and animal welfare.

Fresh Dairy

We offer a range of dairy products, including milk and cheese, produced from cows that are pasture-raised and free of synthetic hormones.

Consultancy Services
